The Great Wall of China

Download
Learning Objective

In this project, our goal was to create an experience where people learn about three different Chinese landmarks while walking along the Great Wall of China.

User Sense of Presence

Music, sound effects and a reasonably realistic landscape all contribute to the user's sense of presence. In addition, the transportation element allows users to "teleport" to certain locations such as the boat and Terracotta Army site in order to get a closer look at the location. In the instance of the Forbidden City, users can actually "walk" around the location before continuing on the Great Wall.
